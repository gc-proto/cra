Pay online

Online banking

Processing times: Typically 1 to 3 business days

Make a payment or schedule future payments to the CRA through your Canadian financial institution

Interac Debit, Visa Debit, or Debit Mastercard (using
CRA My Payment)

Processing times: Typically same or next business day

CRA My Payment is a service that allows you use a debit card to make online payments directly to the CRA (does not include credit cards)

Pre-authorized debit (PAD)

Processing times: Typically 1 to 3 business days (first payment takes an additional 5 days)

Schedule future payments to authorize the CRA to withdraw amounts from your Canadian bank account

Credit card, PayPal, or Interac e-transfer

Processing times: Varies based on service provider

Only available through the third-party service providers, PaySimply and Plastiq (service fees apply)

Third-party service providers

Processing times: Varies based on service provider

Make a payment through select independent third-party service providers (service fees apply)

Wire transfer

Processing times: Typically 1 to 3 business days (considered paid once received by the CRA)

For non-residents who need to make a payment to the CRA and do not have a Canadian bank account

Not accepted online

  • Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin)
  • Gift card
  • Cash

Know how to recognize a scam.

Pay in person

Financial institution (cheque, debit, or wire transfer)

Processing times: Typically 1 to 3 business days (considered paid as of the date stamped)

Pay at your Canadian bank, financial institution, or credit union using a printed personalized remittance voucher

Canada Post location (debit or cash)

Processing times: Typically 1 to 3 business days (considered paid as of the date stamped)

Pay at a Canada Post location with a QR code, to apply your payment to the right CRA account (service fees apply)

CRA drop box location (cheque or money order)

Processing times: Typically 10 business days (considered paid as of the date stamped)

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, only 3 drop boxes locations are open: Jonquière, Sudbury, and Winnipeg tax centres

International financial institution (wire transfer)

Processing times: Typically 1 to 3 business (considered paid once received by the CRA)

For non-residents who need to make a payment to the CRA and do not have a Canadian bank account

Pay by mail

Cheque or money order

Processing times: Typically 10 business days (considered paid once received by the CRA)

Mail your remittance voucher with your Canadian cheque or money order payment

Not accepted by mail

  • Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin)
  • Gift card
  • Cash

Know how to recognize a scam.

To follow-up on a payment you made, refer to: Confirm your payment is received.
