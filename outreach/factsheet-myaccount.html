Register for My Account

What is My Account?

My Account is your one-stop online Canada Revenue Agency service channel. It allows you to:

  • track your refund,
  • view or change your return,
  • check your benefit and credit payments,
  • change your address,
  • view your RRSP limit,
  • set up direct deposit,
  • receive mail notifications,
  • automatically fill in parts of your tax return,
  • and so much more!

To register go to canada.ca/my-cra-account

  1. Choose 1 of 2 ways to register for My Account:
    • using one of our Sign-In Partners
    • creating a CRA user ID & password
  2. To register you will need your:
    • social insurance number
    • date of birth
    • current postal code or zip code
    • current and previous tax returns
  3. Enter your CRA security code
    Once you receive your CRA security code, return to My Account, log in and enter your security code when prompted.

You now have full access to all the My Account features!
