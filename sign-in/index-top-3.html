Register for My Account

You have 3 options to register for My Account with the CRA available on:

Option 1: Register through your bank or financial institution

Before you can register using this option, you must have filed your income tax and benefit return for the current tax year or the previous one.

You must have an online account with one of the Government of Canada's sign in partner banks or financial institutions listed.

  1. Select "Sign-In Partner" under Option 1 on My Account for Individuals
  2. Select your financial institution and enter your credentials with that institution
  3. You will then be asked to provide your:
    • social insurance number
    • postal code
    • date of birth
    • a line amount from your previous year personal income tax return
  4. While you wait for a security code to be sent to you by mail, you can see your TFSA and RRSP contribution room with a one-time access code
  5. Once you receive the security code, you can finish your registration by signing into My Account

Option 2: Register through a CRA user ID

Before you can register using this option, you must have filed your income tax and benefit return for the current tax year or the previous one.

To register for a CRA user ID:

  1. Select "CRA register" under Option 2 on My Account for Individuals
  2. You will be asked to provide your:
    • social insurance number
    • postal code
    • date of birth
    • a line amount from your previous year personal income tax return
  3. While you wait for a security code to be sent to you by mail, you can see your TFSA and RRSP contribution room with a one-time access code
  4. Once you receive the security code, you can finish your registration by signing into My Account

Option 3: Register through your MyAlberta Digital ID or BC Services Card

If you already have a MyAlberta Digital ID or BS Services Card, you will only need to:

  1. select "Provincial partner sign in" under Option 3 on My Account for Individuals
  2. agree to the privacy conditions of the partner sign in service
  3. enter your credentials with your province to access My Account
