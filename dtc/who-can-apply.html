Find out if you are eligible

Eligibility is based on the effects of a mental or physical impairment rather than a diagnosis or the presence of a condition. Find out if your situation may qualify you for the DTC.

Find out if you should apply

Answer a few questions to find out if your situation might qualify.

        ○ insulin therapy
        ○ oxygen therapy
        ○ chest physiotherapy
