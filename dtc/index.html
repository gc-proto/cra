Disability Tax Credit (DTC)

The Disability tax credit (DTC) is a non-refundable credit that helps people with severe impairments reduce the amount of income tax they, or their caregivers, have to pay.

Individuals may apply for the credit, and once they are approved, claim the credit on their tax return.

By reducing the amount of income tax you pay, the DTC aims to offset some of the extra costs of care, treatment and related activities.
