Disability tax credit (DTC)

The Disability tax credit (DTC) is a non-refundable tax credit that helps people with severe impairments or their caregivers reduce the amount of income tax they may have to pay.

Individuals may apply for the credit, and if they are approved, claim the credit on their tax return.

By reducing the amount of income tax you pay, the DTC aims to offset some of the extra costs of care, treatment and related activities.
