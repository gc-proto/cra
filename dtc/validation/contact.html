Contact the CRA

Online

Use your CRA My Account to:

  • get tax credit information
  • send new documentation using "Submit documents" in the top right corner
Sign in to My Account Register

By phone

Call the CRA by phone or TTY if you have:

  • enquiries about the DTC
  • questions about your CRA Account
Before you call

To confirm your identity, you'll need your:

  • Social Insurance Number (SIN)
  • full name and date of birth
  • complete address
  • assessed tax return, notice of assessment or reassessment, other tax document, or be signed in to CRA My Account

If you’re calling on behalf of someone else, we will need to get their authorization and confirm their identity when you call.

For details: Authorize a representative
