Get ready to apply

Gather all the information

Only complete applications will be considered by the Voluntary Disclosures Program (VDP). A complete application will include:

  • The completed and signed VDP application form (RC199 ), or an equivalent letter
  • All necessary returns, forms and schedules
  • Payment of the estimated amount of the taxes owing

When to apply

You can apply for the VDP at any time of the year. It is important to know that you have to apply before you file your tax return or amendments.

Fill out the application or send a letter

To apply to the VDP, it is best to use Form RC199, Voluntary Disclosures Program (VDP) Application.

You can send a letter with the same information requested on the form and make sure it meets the following requirements:

  • The information requested in sections 1 and 2 of the Form RC199, must be included
  • If applicable to your situation, the information requested in sections 3 to 6 of Form RC199, must be included
  • Your letter must indicate that you, and your representative if applicable, have read and agreed to the Taxpayer Declaration in Section 7 of Form RC199
  • The letter must be signed by you and, if applicable, the representative submitting the application on your behalf

Submit the application

You may submit your application online, through fax, or by mail.

Online

Submit your application with 1 of the CRA sign-in services:

  • For individuals: My account
  • For businesses: My Business account
  • For representatives: Represent a client

Representatives must be authorized by filling in the Authorize a representative.
