  4. Know the due dates

    Summary of the information or tasks that can be accomplished on the topic page.

    Key dates for your 2020 tax return

    • Feb 22, 2021: Earliest you can file online
    • Mar 1, 2021: Deadline to contribute to a RRSP, PRPP or SPP
    • Apr 30, 2021: Deadline to pay your taxes (applies to everyone)
    • Apr 30, 2021: Deadline to file your taxes
    • Jun 15, 2021: Deadline to file your taxes (if you’re self-employed or have a spouse or a common-law partner who is self-employed)

      Refund processing time after your taxes are assessed
      • Direct deposit: About 3 to 5 business days.
      • Cheque: About 10 to 12 business days.
        However, due to COVID-19 health and safety protocol, a cheque may be further delayed. This is because a cheque comes into contact with many surfaces and individuals in order to be delivered to you.
